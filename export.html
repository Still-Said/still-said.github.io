Send it to us
In the To field, type stillsaid@proton.me. For the subject line, just your name is fine. Then hit send. The chat file is already attached — you're done.
The file goes directly into an encrypted inbox that only we can access. We delete it as soon as your keepsake is ready.

One small thing: please send from the same email address you used on the intake form — it helps us match everything up.

Your archive is processed privately. Nothing is stored after your keepsake is generated. We never train on your data.
